Our Story

Njuri Senior School (formerly Njuri High School) is a C2 Public Mixed Boarding School located in Magumoni Location, Meru South Sub-County, Tharaka-Nithi County.

Formerly known as Njuri High School, the institution was upgraded to a Senior School under Kenya's new Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) framework â€” now serving Grade 10, 11, and 12 learners through three CBC pathways: STEM, Social Sciences, and Arts & Sports.

Under the leadership of Principal Rutere Henry Mwenda, the school has grown to an enrollment of 925 students, continuing its proud tradition of academic excellence and community service in Tharaka-Nithi County.
