Our Story

Chuka Girls Secondary School, officially known as St. Bakhita Chuka Girls High School, is a premier Extra County girls boarding school located in Karingari, Chuka, Tharaka-Nithi County, Kenya.

The school is committed to providing quality education in a safe, nurturing environment that empowers young women to achieve their full academic and personal potential.

With over 700 students and 30 qualified teachers, we offer a comprehensive curriculum covering Sciences, Humanities, Languages, and Technical subjects — all tailored to prepare students for KCSE and beyond.
