SPORTS & ATHLETICS

Champions on the Field

Our students compete at sub-county, county and national levels

Volleyball

A regular competitor in Tharaka-Nithi County games, advancing consistently to regional rounds.

Athletics

Track and field events including long distance, relay, and field events. Athletes have represented Tharaka-Nithi County nationally.

Football

An active team participating in inter-school competitions across the Mt. Kenya East region.

Aerobics

Physical fitness and discipline through structured aerobics and inter-school competitions.

MUSIC, DRAMA & ARTS

Where Talent Meets the Stage

Njuri Senior School has a proud tradition in Kenya's national Music & Cultural Festival

School Choir

Our choir competes annually in the Kenya Music Festival, performing choral verse, set books, and traditional songs at county and regional levels.

Drama Club

Participates in the Kenya National Drama Festival. Students perform original and set-book plays, developing confidence and creative expression.

Fine Art & Craft

Students pursue visual arts, craft, and design as both a subject and co-curricular activity. Annual art exhibitions showcase creativity across all forms.

CLUBS & SOCIETIES

Faith, Service & Leadership

Christian Union (C.U)

One of the most active clubs. Members meet weekly for Bible study, worship, and prayer. The C.U participates in county-level competitions and organizes school-wide spiritual events.

Environment Club

Dedicated to keeping the compound clean and green. Organizes tree planting, clean-up campaigns, and the annual Environment Day.

Student Council

The elected student leadership body that represents students to the administration. Council members develop real leadership and communication skills.

ACADEMIC CLUBS

Learning Beyond the Timetable

Science Club

Experiments, science fairs, and STEM competitions at county and national level.

Debate Club

Public speaking and inter-school debate competitions. Builds confidence and critical thinking.

Literature Club

Reading, creative writing, and spoken word poetry. Complements English and Kiswahili.

Mathematics Club

Problem-solving competitions, peer tutoring and mathematics olympiad preparation.
